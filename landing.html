Our Solution

Parking in 3 Taps

A mobile-first platform that removes all friction — from discovery to payment.

1

📍 Find

See available spots on a live GPS map, sorted by distance. Real-time status (free / occupied). Haversine-accurate distances. One tap for Google Maps directions.

2

📅 Book

Reserve your spot instantly. Choose duration (1–8 hrs). Your space is held while you drive there. No phone calls. No agents. No cash.

3

💳 Pay

Pay via MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, or AirtelTigo Money directly in the app. Funds deducted from wallet. Receipt in-app. Completely cashless.

Technology

Production-Ready from Day One

Built on a modern, scalable stack — already live and accepting real bookings.

🗺️
Real-Time Map

Leaflet.js + OpenStreetMap with live GPS markers. Haversine distance sorting. One-tap Google Maps directions.

💳
Mobile Money

Paystack integration — MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo. OTP flow. Idempotent payment records prevent double-charging.
